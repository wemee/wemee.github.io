01 — About

A designer who ships.

I've worked as a lead graphic designer across agencies and in-house teams in Taiwan and Canada. My work runs from brand and image design to website and UI design, and the full range of marketing creative — campaigns, EDM, DM, posters and social ads.

I'm fluent across the Adobe Creative Suite and Sketch, build responsive layouts in HTML5 and CSS3, and partner closely with engineering to ship the design rather than just hand it off. Educated at NTHU in Information & Communication Media; based in Taiwan.
